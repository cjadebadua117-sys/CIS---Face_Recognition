Face Recognition Attendance

Scan your face to mark attendance

Select your active class on the right and tap scan. The system will use the live camera feed to verify your face.

How to mark attendance

  • The instructor will open the attendance session for your subject.
  • Once the session is active, click Enable Camera to open your webcam.
  • If the session is active, click Mark Present while the camera is on.
  • If you are late, click Mark Late during the late window.
  • After the session time ends, attendance closes automatically.
